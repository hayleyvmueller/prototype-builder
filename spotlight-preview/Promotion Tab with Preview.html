Spotlight Listings

Elevate your listing to the first page of relevant search results on Realtor.com. Gain greater visibility, attract more interested buyers, and sell faster.

How Spotlight Listings work

Step 1
Create your promotion

To start a promotion, click "Promote this listing" above.

If you have pre-paid promotions from a subscription or package, select Apply promotion, then Launch promotion at the bottom of the screen.

Once you've used all of your pre-paid promotions, you can still launch additional promotions at any time for an extra cost.

Step 2
View performance

Easily track your promotion's engagement metrics, such as views and clicks, directly from the Promotions tab.
